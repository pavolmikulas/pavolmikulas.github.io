The goal of this project was to design a trophy for the national winners of the IPA competition Deeper Perspective category.

It encapsulates prestige, honour and recognition. The trophy was meant to become a desirable object for its meaning in the world of photography as well as its value as a design object.

Things should be made of stuff they are proposed for. The carafe is inspired by water and looks as made out of it, so the trophy should represent ambition.

I designed the trophy in cooperation with the Olgoj Chorchoj studio in 2006.

It was produced by BeldaFactory and Moser glassworks.

Model J is a full suspension balance bike for children aged from 2 to 4 years. Model J is made of birch plywood, glued together with waterproof glue and then painted in two layers with quality non toxic coating. It is light weight, easy to assemble and easy to use.

Model J is the only full suspension balance bike on the market. The suspension is ensured by a patented system, LVS. The system was created by Jiří Marján, who produces Model J for his brand Re pello in the Czech Republic.

Thanks to the suspension system vibrations are significantly reduced when riding on any surface. The suspension system protects the child's body from harmful vibrations and provides a comfortable riding experience.

Re pello Model J was exhibited at Prague’s Designblok 2015 and 16 and at the exhibition Salone del Mobile 2017 in Milan.

Model J differs from the other balance bikes because of it's functionality and beautiful design. With Model J children will be moving happily through their chilhood.

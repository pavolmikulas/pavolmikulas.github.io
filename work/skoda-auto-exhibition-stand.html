The exhibition stand has a metal base covered with an elastic fabric. The purpose of the exhibition stand is to attract the attention of city-goers. It's elegant, eyecatching design helps display the car and to carry the feel of the Škoda brand.

It was exhibited at Prague’s Designblok'07 at Jan Palach Square and also at Designblok'08. It was designed in cooperation with Škoda Auto Design.

The Škoda leather collection is a set of wallets, a manicure set and a keychain. I concentrated on designing a product which promotes quality in the company.

It was an honor to work for Škoda Auto, a well known automobile manufacturing company, one of the oldest car making companies in the world.

Good cooperation with an open minded producer led to this set of unique products reflecting positive thinking and can do attitude. I give special thanks to Uniko for helping this design come to life.

It is produced by Uniko. Products are available at Škoda Auto Stores.

This simple chair is made from beech with wood bending technology. I designed it in 2008.

The chair makes an excelent pair with the Walnut table. I wanted to create a chair that complemented the Walnut table.

It is a simple and undistinguished chair but still elegant and comfortable.

It was exhibited at Prague’s Designblok in 2008.
